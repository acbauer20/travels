Beaches

The backgroud picture is of a beach in Bermuda. The sand is really pink and my mother took us to the beach almost every day. As far as I know it is the only pink sand in the world. I would like to go back and see it again.




Portugal, Greece and Aruba: three very beautiful places that I have visited. Each offers it's own beauty, it's own tranquility. I have sunned on these beaches, I have swam in these waters. I have partaken in the peace.
